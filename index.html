Backstory

My origin story? Think less "fairy-tale rescue" and more "strategic positioning." In the summer of 2023, I was casually haunting a hair salon in Changsha, China — accepting handouts, cultivating mystique — when my human walked in for a bleach job and apparently decided I was a better investment than highlights. She wasn't wrong.

I was, at the time, unusually emaciated — a fact I attribute entirely to the salon's uninspired menu. My human noticed, took me home, and the rest is history. I am a moyen poodle, or possibly a standard who simply decided full-size was unnecessary effort — the jury is still out. Born around 2021, my early years were spent honing the art of survival on the streets of Changsha.

I now live indoors. It suits me.
